Programs and

National Chamber of Ecotourism and Sustainable Tourism (CANAECO)

Non-governmental, non-profit, and private character organization. Promotes the development of the tourist activity in the country.

Code of
Conduct

Green Flag Program or Sanitary Quality Seal)

Award granted to aims and promote the participation of civil society to improve the operation and maintenance of the aqueducts.

National Chamber of Tourism

Non-profit organization, which brings together the chambers and associations of the tourist business in the country.

Costa Rica SMEs seal (PYME)

Committee representing the community of Monteverde before State institutions, in topics related to the construction of the route 606.

Ecological Blue Flag Program

Annually given award, it rewards efforts in accordance with the protection of natural resources and the implementation of actions.

Designed to differentiate tourism businesses according to the degree in which its operation and functioning is approaching a model of sustainability.
